Bare dirt — let's begin.
📖 New to this? The whole method in 30 seconds

The 3 moves (your one day a month): ① Whack — cut down what's finished, leave it on the soil. ② Feed — scatter a thin layer of compost on top. ③ Plant — poke in the next seeds. The other 29 days you just pick and eat.

The soil secret: you don't fix your yard's dirt — you fill the bed with good soil and garden above it. Sandy, clay, wrong-pH? Doesn't matter; roots never touch it.

Scared you'll kill it? You start small. If something flops, you're out a $30 weekend, not your dream. Plants want to grow — your job is just not getting in the way.

Heads up: dates are typical for your climate, not gospel — local weather shifts things a week or two. Watch the sky, trust your finger in the soil (dry 2 inches down = water), and you'll be fine.
